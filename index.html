Frequently Asked Questions

What are your operating hours? +
We're open Monday to Friday, 7:00 AM to 6:00 PM, year-round except major holidays.
What is your teacher-to-child ratio? +
We maintain ratios that exceed standards: Infants 1:4, Toddlers 1:6, Kindergarten 1:8.
Do you provide meals? +
Yes, we provide nutritious breakfast, lunch, and snacks daily. All meals accommodate dietary restrictions and preferences.
What curriculum do you follow? +
We use a child-centered, play-based approach that promotes emotional, social, physical, and cognitive development through structured and experiential learning.
Can I visit before enrolling? +
Absolutely! We encourage tours of our facility. Contact us to schedule a visit and meet our team.
Do you offer part-time enrollment? +
Yes, we offer both full-time (5 days) and part-time (2-3 days) options depending on availability.
