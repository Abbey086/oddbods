Our Programs

Tailored learning experiences for every stage of your child's early development

Ages 6-18 Months

Infant Care

üçº Gentle Beginnings

Our infant care program provides a safe, nurturing environment where your baby's earliest experiences are filled with warmth, comfort, and gentle exploration. We focus on building secure attachments and supporting your baby's natural development through responsive caregiving.

Each infant receives individualized attention with low ratios (1:4), ensuring their unique needs, schedules, and temperaments are honored. Our caring educators create a home-like atmosphere where babies feel loved and secure.

Program Highlights

  • Individualized care routines and flexible scheduling
  • Sensory exploration with safe, age-appropriate materials
  • Language-rich interactions through songs and conversation
  • Gentle introduction to social play with peers
  • Tummy time and movement activities for motor development
  • Daily communication with parents about routines and milestones

Ages 18 Months - 3 Years

Toddler Program

üß∏ Curious Explorers

Our toddler program celebrates this exciting age of discovery and independence. We provide a stimulating environment where toddlers can safely explore, experiment, and begin developing important social and self-help skills.

Through play-based learning and hands-on activities, toddlers develop language, fine and gross motor skills, and begin to understand their emotions and the world around them. Our patient, supportive educators guide children through this dynamic stage of growth.

Program Highlights

  • Language development through stories, songs, and conversations
  • Independence-building through self-help skills (feeding, dressing)
  • Early socialization and learning to share and take turns
  • Creative play with art, music, and dramatic play
  • Large motor skill development through active play
  • Potty training support in partnership with families

Ages 3-4 Years

Nursery

üé® Playful Learners

Our nursery program builds foundational skills through engaging, play-based learning. Children explore early literacy, numeracy, science, and creative arts while developing strong social-emotional skills and independence.

We create rich learning environments with hands-on centers that encourage exploration, problem-solving, and creativity. Children learn to work cooperatively, express themselves, and develop confidence in their abilities.

Program Highlights

  • Pre-literacy skills through storytelling and phonics activities
  • Early math concepts with manipulatives and games
  • Science discovery through nature exploration and experiments
  • Creative arts including painting, crafts, music, and dance
  • Social skills development through group activities and play
  • Introduction to classroom routines and learning structures

Ages 4-5 Years

Kindergarten Prep

üéì Confident Students

Our kindergarten program prepares children for primary school success through a balanced curriculum that emphasizes academic readiness, critical thinking, and strong character development. Children build confidence as capable, curious learners.

We focus on developing essential skills in literacy, numeracy, problem-solving, and collaboration while nurturing creativity and a love of learning. Our structured yet playful approach ensures children are academically, socially, and emotionally prepared for the next step.

Program Highlights

  • Early reading and writing with phonics and sight words
  • Math skills including counting, patterns, and simple operations
  • STEM activities promoting critical thinking and problem-solving
  • Project-based learning on topics of interest
  • Leadership skills and responsibility through classroom jobs
  • Preparation for primary school routines and expectations

A Day in Our Program

Every day is thoughtfully structured to balance learning, play, rest, and nutrition

7:00 - 9:00 AM
Arrival & Free Play
Warm welcome, breakfast, and child-directed play in learning centers
9:00 - 9:30 AM
Circle Time
Songs, calendar, weather, group discussion, and introduction to daily themes
9:30 - 11:00 AM
Learning Centers
Literacy, math, science, art stations with teacher-guided small group activities
11:00 - 11:45 AM
Outdoor Play
Active play, nature exploration, games, and gross motor development
11:45 AM - 12:30 PM
Lunch Time
Family-style dining, social skills practice, and healthy eating habits
12:30 - 2:30 PM
Rest & Quiet Time
Nap time for younger children, quiet activities and reading for older children
2:30 - 4:00 PM
Afternoon Activities
Music, movement, STEM projects, dramatic play, and creative exploration
4:00 - 6:00 PM
Snack & Departure
Afternoon snack, free play, outdoor time, and flexible pick-up
